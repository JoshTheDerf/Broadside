How to Play Broadside

Two players share one device, sitting across from each other. Player 1 sits at the bottom, Player 2 at the top. In Solo Play, you're P1 and the AI controls P2.

Setup

In Custom Setup, players alternate placing terrain, then deploy ships in their home zone. In Quick/Solo Play, setup is automatic.

On Your Turn

Tap one of your ships to select it. A menu appears with three actions:

⚓ Move — A movement ring appears. Tap anywhere to sail to that point on the ring. Your ship rotates to face its new heading.

💥 Fire — Cannons fire from the sides of the ship (broadside!). Choose Port or Starboard, set Bearing (fore/aft angle) and Elevation (range). Ships cannot fire directly forward or backward — positioning matters!

🃏 Card — Play a crew card from your hand. Tap a card, then tap a target ship.

Each ship gets one action per turn. Tap End Turn when done.

Cards

You hold 3 crew cards. To play one: tap the card in your hand, then tap the target ship on the board. You draw back to 3 at the end of your turn.

⚡ cards are free actions — they don't use up a ship's turn. They glow gold.

Card Reference

🔭 Lookout ⚡ — Shows the distance to the nearest enemy. Tap one of your ships.

🛡️ Brace for Impact — Next hit on this ship deals 1 less damage. Tap one of your ships.

🚩 Signal Flags ⚡ — Gives an allied ship a bonus action, even if it already acted. Tap one of your ships.

⛵ Full Sail — Extended movement range with multiple distance rings. Tap one of your ships, then tap a destination.

↔️ Evasive Maneuvers — Slide sideways without changing facing. Tap one of your ships, then tap perpendicular.

🎯 Skilled Gunner — Fire twice from one ship. Tap one of your ships, then aim and fire twice.

🔧 Repair Crew — Restore 1 HP (a mast reappears). Tap one of your damaged ships.

⚔️ Boarding Party — Deal 1 damage to a nearby enemy. You must have a ship adjacent. Tap the enemy ship.

👁️ Spotter — Ghost shot to see where it lands, then real shot. Tap one of your ships, then aim twice.

💨 Smokescreen ⚡ — 50% chance enemies miss through smoke. Lasts 1 turn. Tap one of your ships.

Terrain

Islands and rocks block movement and cannonball paths. Reefs are cosmetic. Ships cannot sail through blocking terrain.

Victory

Sink all 3 of your opponent's ships! Each ship has 3 HP (shown as masts). Hit a ship to break a mast.
